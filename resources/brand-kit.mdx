## Usage Guidelines

When using Soldexer brand assets, please follow these guidelines:

1. **Do not modify the logos**: Please do not alter the colors, proportions, or elements of our logos.
2. **Clear space**: Always maintain adequate clear space around our logo to ensure visibility and impact.
3. **Sizing**: Never display the logo at a size where it becomes illegible or loses detail.
4. **Background**: Ensure there is sufficient contrast between the logo and the background.
